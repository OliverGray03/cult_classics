About us

We are an online vintage clothing store, offering original products of football teams from all over the world, dating back to the 1980s.

We have a passion for both football and retro sportswear, and by combining the two, we are proud to bring you on-trend vintage attire to help re-live those iconic football memories. We aim to scour the globe to find rare, authentic garments from the previous few decades, and make them acquirable once again for our customers to wear and collect.

We understand, you want the exact shirt you once owned as a child, or from the season your team won the cup. Well this is where we come in!

We want to have complete transparency with our customers, so if you have any questions please do not hesitate to contact us.
